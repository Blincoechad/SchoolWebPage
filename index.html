Welcome to Our Sanctuary

At Ott's Off-Leash Dog Sanctuary, we provide a safe, spacious, and stimulating environment where your beloved dogs can run, play, and socialize freely. Our sanctuary is dedicated to giving every dog the freedom they deserve in a secure and supervised setting.

With acres of open space, your furry friends can explore, exercise, and enjoy the great outdoors while making new friends. We believe that happy dogs make happy families, and we're here to make that happen every day.

Our Facilities

Our state-of-the-art facilities are designed with your dog's safety and enjoyment in mind. We feature separate play areas for different dog sizes and temperaments, ensuring every pup has the perfect space to play.

From shaded rest areas to agility equipment, water stations to comfortable seating for pet parents, we've thought of everything to make your visit enjoyable for both you and your four-legged companion.

Safety First

The safety of every dog in our care is our top priority. Our sanctuary is fully fenced and secure, with trained staff monitoring all activities. We maintain strict health and vaccination requirements to ensure a safe environment for all visitors.

Before your first visit, we conduct a temperament assessment to ensure your dog will thrive in our off-leash environment. This helps us create the best experience for your pet and all the dogs in our community.
