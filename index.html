Welcome to Our Sanctuary

Welcome to Ott's Off-Leash Dog Sanctuary, a non-profit organization created by a love for dogs and a vision to provide excellence in canine recreation. -Pott's Dog Sanctuary is not for sale. In fact, it is being legally restructured to become a permanent fixture in our community.

What We Do

Dr. Potts, along with a dedicated team of volunteers is working to maintain and improve the facilities We are committed to creating a safe, clean, and fun environment for dogs and their owners.

We Need You

Remember, as a private facility with NO government funding, Your support is crucial to keep this sanctuary open and thriving. Your tax-free donations help us improve the park's facilities and much more. Please consider a gift of $1.00 per dog for each visit. For example, for one dog visiting us two days per week, a gift of $100 per year would be appropriate.

Enjoy Your Visit!

Please familiarize yourself with the rules outlined in the Regulations Section to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all visitors. The Sanctuary takes these rules seriously, and enforces them for the safety of both dogs and their owners.
